Anyone working with Rainmaker or Rainmaker stationery should take a look at this article. Aside from being a good resource for implementing CSS styles in email campaigns, it helps shine some light on how email clients mangle HTML code and how to best deal with that.

No matter what you do stationery-wise or content-wise, you're doing it in HTML, and it's good to be familiar with that.
